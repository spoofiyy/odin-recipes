Chicken Rice Meal

Southern style rice meal with chicken that comes with variety of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Letuce
  • Gravy
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker for 25 minutes seasoned with turmeric
  2. Marinate chicken with honey, garlic, salt, and pepper
  3. Make gravy
  4. Wash lettuce
  5. Put together
  6. Serve
