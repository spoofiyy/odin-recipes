Lasagna

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Italian Seasoning
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
